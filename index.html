FAQ

  • What's Hack Day?

    Hack day is a smaller, 24-hour UCSD only hackathon hosted by SD Hacks focused on mentorship, learning, and shipping cool projects.

  • Where and When?

    This Hack Day will be held in the Qualcomm Room in Jacobs Hall. Plan on arriving at 5:00 PM Friday and departing 6:30 PM Saturday.

  • What should I bring?

    Bring your student ID, computer, any external peripherals you may need (chargers, monitors, etc.), any other hardware that you definitely plan on using, and an open mind.

  • What if I don't have a team?

    We allow teams of up to four hackers. Though we recommend signing up with your team, don't worry if you don't have one set because we'll have a team formation session in the beginning!
