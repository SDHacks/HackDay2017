FAQ

  • What's Hack Day?

    Hack day is a smaller, 24-hour UCSD only hackathon hosted by SD Hacks focused on mentorship, learning, and shipping cool projects.

  • Where and When?

    This Hack Day will be held in the Qualcomm Room in Jacobs Hall. Plan on arriving at 5:00 PM Friday 1/13 and departing 6:30 PM Saturday 1/14.

  • What should I bring?

    Bring your student ID, computer, any peripherals you may need (chargers, monitors, etc.), any hardware that you plan on using, and an open mind.

  • Do I need a team to sign up?

    Yes, since Hack Days are smaller events, we want to ensure everyone who comes is able to work with a team. Signing up with a team allows us to accept the people you'd like to work with without leaving any stragglers behind! Teams of 2-4 are allowed.

  • Are there any rules?

    As a Major League Hacking member event, we are adopting Major League Hacking's Code of Conduct which requires you to respect others and help us keep Hack Day a safe space for everyone. If you want your project to be considered for an award, you may only submit work that your team wrote during the hackathon.

  • Have an unanswered question?

    Feel free to email us at hello@sdhacks.io
